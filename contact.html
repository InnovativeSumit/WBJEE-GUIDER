FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW DOES THE TFW QUOTA WORK IN WBJEE?

THE TUITION FEE WAIVER (TFW) SCHEME ALLOWS MERITORIOUS STUDENTS FROM ECONOMICALLY WEAKER SECTIONS TO SECURE ADMISSION IN ENGINEERING COLLEGES WITHOUT PAYING TUITION FEES. UNDER THIS SCHEME, 5% OF SEATS IN ALL PARTICIPATING INSTITUTES ARE RESERVED. ELIGIBILITY IS BASED ON WBJEE RANK AND FAMILY INCOME CRITERIA, ENSURING ACCESS TO QUALITY EDUCATION FOR DESERVING CANDIDATES.

WHAT'S THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN YOUR FREE AND PAID CONSULTATIONS?

OUR FREE 15-MINUTE CONSULTATION INCLUDES ONLY A CALL WITH YOU TO DISCUSS YOUR WBJEE SITUATION AND BASIC GUIDANCE. IN THE PAID CONSULTATION, WE ANALYZE YOUR RANK AND MAKE DETAILED CHOICE FILLING SHEETS PERSONALLY TAILORED FOR YOU, HELPING YOU SELECT THE BEST COLLEGES AND BRANCHES.

CAN YOU GUARANTEE ADMISSION TO A SPECIFIC COLLEGE?

WE CREATE YOUR CHOICE FILLINGS STRICTLY BASED ON YOUR RANK TO MAXIMIZE YOUR CHANCES OF GETTING THE BEST POSSIBLE COLLEGES. HOWEVER, FINAL ADMISSION DEPENDS ON VARIOUS FACTORS INCLUDING VACANCIES, CUTOFF FLUCTUATIONS, AND YOUR LUCK — SO WE TRY TO GUARANTEE ADMISSION TO A SPECIFIC COLLEGE.

HOW EARLY SHOULD I START WBJEE COUNSELING?

WE RECOMMEND STARTING YOUR STRATEGY AS SOON AS WBJEE RESULTS ARE ANNOUNCED. EARLY PREPARATION ALLOWS AMPLE TIME FOR RESEARCHING OPTIONS, PREPARING DOCUMENTATION, AND DEVELOPING BACKUP PLANS. HOWEVER, OUR TEAM CAN ASSIST AT ANY STAGE OF THE COUNSELING PROCESS.
